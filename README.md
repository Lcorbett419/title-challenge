# Title Challenge - Football Manager

A text-based football manager game where you manage your team, buy and sell players, and compete to win the league!

## Play Now
Visit: https://lcorbett419.github.io/title-challenge

## Features
- Team management
- Player transfers
- Text-based match commentary
- League table tracking
- Database editor

## How to Play
1. Choose your team
2. Manage your squad
3. Buy and sell players
4. Play matches and climb the league table!
